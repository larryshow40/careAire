Our Pilot Training Courses

Private Pilot Training

The private pilot license is divided into two components, ground school and flight training.

  • You must be 17 years old
  • Must have FAA Medical Certificate
  • Minimum of 40 hours of training.
  • Takes 3-6 months to complete depending on student

Commercial Training

We offer unique FAA approved Part 141 Commercial Pilot Certification Course that allows you get your licence for only 90hrs!

  • You must be 18 years old
  • private pilot certificate
  • Must have FAA medical certificate
  • Instrument Rating
  • Read, Speak and understand English.

Aircaft Mechanics

We train technicians to maintain and repair airplanes and jets to meet FAA standards

  • You must be 18 years old
  • Read, Speak and understand English.

Instrutment Rating

Instrument rating allow students to obtain the knowledge, skill, and aeronautical experience

  • You must be 18 years old
  • Private or Commercial pilot certificate
  • Must have FAA medical certificate
  • Read, Speak and understand English.
